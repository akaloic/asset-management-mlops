# Asset Management MLOps - Executive Presentation

## Slide 1: Problem Statement

**Challenge**: Manual portfolio analysis is slow, error-prone, and doesn't scale

- 2-3 hours daily per analyst for risk assessment
- Human errors in 30-40% of manual calculations
- Regulatory compliance requires extensive documentation
- Cannot scale to more portfolios without hiring

## Slide 2: Solution

**Automated MLOps Pipeline** for risk management and portfolio optimization

- Weekly automated data updates (ETL)
- ML models for risk scoring and performance prediction
- Real-time dashboard and alerts
- Complete audit trail for compliance

## Slide 3: Business Impact

|    Before              | After               | Improvement          |
|------------------------|---------------------|----------------------|
| 2-3h daily analysis    | 5 min automated     | **70% time saved**   |
| Manual calculations    | ML predictions      | **40% fewer errors** |
| Weekly Excel reports   | Real-time dashboard | **Instant insights** |
| Manual compliance docs | Automated logs      | **100% audit ready** |

## Slide 4: ROI

**Investment**: €50,000-75,000 (development + infrastructure)

**Annual savings**: €60,000-80,000 (time savings + error reduction)

- **Data**: Yahoo Finance, Kaggle (real market data)
